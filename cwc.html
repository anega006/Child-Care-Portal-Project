Open Shelter

Open Shelter for Urban and Semi urban areas will cater to all children in need of care and protection like beggars,

street & working children, rag pickers, orphaned, deserted, trafficked children. Particularly those without home & family

ties, vulnerable to abuseand exploitation especially children of sex workers & children of pavement dwellers.And it focuses on the

Enrollment in schools, vocational training, placement, health services, reducing drug & substance abuse and HIV/AIDS in street children.
